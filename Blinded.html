Description

Blinded is a boss rush-focused soul-like action game. Our game explores the theme of color loss. Powerful bosses stole the red, green, and blue colors from the world, leaving it only black and white. The player embarks on a journey to restore the colors to this colorless world. Encountering monsters and bosses, the player will face numerous deaths, but by continuously challenging and defeating bosses while experiencing various patterns, the player will overcome this crisis and bring the color back to the world.

Gameplay

The gameplay can be broken down into three basic elements: Attack, dodge, and survive. Face all the monsters that stand in your way. Slide to dodge or block enemy attacks at the right time to survive and aim for their blind spots to attack. When all the enemies are defeated, the path to the next stage shows its way. There are three worlds, Red, Green, and Blue, and a boss awaits in the final stage of each world. Defeat the bosses and bring back the color to the world. Defeating the boss at the first attempt may not be easy. You have to be resilient and keep trying. Again, do not fear death and get familiar with the enemy's attack patterns. Come up with your own strategy and use that as a weapon to conquer them. That's the essence of the gameplay of Blinded.
